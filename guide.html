üîí Getting Started (One-Time Setup)

To use the app for the first time, you'll need to create an account.

  1. Go to the website.
  2. Click the "Sign Up" tab at the top.
  3. Enter your Email Address.
  4. Choose a Password you'll remember.
  5. Enter our special Invite Code (ask the organizer for this!).
  6. Click "Create Account".

üìÖ 4. How to Schedule a Meeting

Once you‚Äôve found a book the club wants to read, you can schedule it so it shows up on everyone's Dashboard with the meeting details.

Step 1: Find and Save the Book

Use the "Find Books" section to search for the book (for example, "James" by Percival Everett) and click "Save to Library."

Step 2: Open the Book in the Library

Go to your "Library" and click on the book to open its details.

Step 3: Fill in the Meeting Details

In the window that pops up, change the Status to "Scheduled." Then, type in the Host Name, pick a Target Date, and set the Meeting Time.

Step 4: Save Your Changes

Click the "Update Details" button at the bottom. The book will now move to the "Scheduled" section of our Library and appear on the Dashboard for everyone to see!

Mission 4: Try scheduling a "Test Book" for a future date. Set yourself as the host and pick a time. Go back to the Dashboard to see if it appeared in the "Coming Up Later" list at the bottom!

‚≠ê 5. Updating Ratings and Tags

You can keep each book's information fresh by fetching latest ratings and adding descriptive tags.

How to Fetch a Rating

When you open a book modal, you may see an old rating or none at all. Click the "Refresh Rating" (or "Get Rating") button under the book cover. The app will search for the latest score and update it for you!

How to Manage Tags

Tags help us organize our books by genre or topic (like "Classic" or "Must Read").

  • To Remove a Tag: Click the tiny "x" or info icon on a tag.
  • To Add a Tag: Type your own tag into the "Add tag..." box and press Enter on your keyboard.
  • To Get Suggestions: Click "Suggest Tags" and the app will automatically add relevant categories for that book!
Mission 5: Open any book in the Library. Try clicking "Refresh Rating" to see it update. Then, add a new tag like "Highly Recommended" and save your changes!

üé® 6. Managing Discussion Guides

Our app can help you prepare for book club by generating discussion questions using AI!

How to Generate a Guide

  1. Open a book's details in the Library.
  2. Click the "Discussion Guide" button (or scroll down to the bottom).
  3. Click the purple "Generate Questions with AI" button.
  4. Wait a few seconds, and a list of questions will appear!

How to Edit Your Questions

If you want to add your own questions or remove some, click "Edit Questions". You can then type directly into the box. When you're done, click "Save Changes".

How to Print or Download

  • Export to Word: Click this button to download the questions as a Microsoft Word document.
  • Print: Click this to print the questions directly from your browser.
Mission 6: Find a book you like, generate AI questions for it, and then try clicking "Export to Word". See if the file opens correctly on your device!

What is the difference between "Find Books" and "Library"?

  • Find Books: This is like looking through a giant catalog for the whole world. Use this to suggest new books to the club.
  • Library: This is our club's personal bookshelf. It only shows books we've already discussed, scheduled, or saved as possibilities.
